I asked several leading AI models to evaluate NYC mayoral candidates Andrew Cuomo and Zohran Mamdani on their housing production policies. The results reveal interesting patterns in how AI systems reason about urban policy tradeoffs.

This analysis explores:
- How different AI models evaluate housing policy proposals
- The role of market mechanisms vs. public housing in AI reasoning
- Limitations of using AI for policy analysis
- What these results tell us about AI alignment with different political philosophies
